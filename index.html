Flojet Triplex 12V 11 LPM 03526144 Caravan RV Boat Fresh Water Pump


Enhance Your Caravan Water Supply with the NEW Flojet Triplex Water Pump!

Discover the Power of the Flojet FJ100 Caravan / RV Freshwater Pump!

Get ready to enjoy uninterrupted freshwater flow with the Flojet Triplex 11 L/Min 12-volt Water Pump. Whether you're exploring the great outdoors in your caravan, camper, or RV, this pump is your perfect companion.

Key Features to Boost Your Adventures:

  • Impressive Flow Rate: Delivering a whopping 11 liters per minute (3 GPM).
  • Consistent Pressure: Equipped with a 50PSI pressure switch for a steady flow.
  • Self-Priming: Draws water from up to 2 meters below.
  • Hassle-Free Setup: Includes 4 plug-in hose port fittings.
  • Quiet Operation: Thanks to soft noise-absorbing mounts.
  • Easy Installation: Snap-in port fittings make it a breeze.
  • Reduced Pulsation: Triplex diaphragm design with an in-built bypass for smoother water delivery.
  • Built to Last: Crafted from corrosion-resistant materials.
  • Safety Assured: Meets ISO 8846 ignition protection standards.
  • Efficient: Low amp draw at 10PSI, just 4.4 amps.
  • Compact Design: Approximately 230mm Long X 110 mm Wide X 110mm high.
  • Peace of Mind: Backed by a 2 Year Warranty.
